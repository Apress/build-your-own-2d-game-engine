Yomi Defense

Yomi defense is a spin on a tower defense styled game where strategy between two players is involved. Yomi meaning "knowing the mind of the opponent", fake out your oponent with strategically placed waypoints or in reverse roles, carefully place your defending towers. Score points based on the amount of enemey spawn get to your opponents base!
